About Me — My Story

Grace T. Sugian is a determined and vibrant 19-year-old student from Mangagoy, Bislig City. As the middle child in her family, she grew up alongside her older sister and younger brother. Despite her parents' separation, Grace has found strength and support in her loving mother, with whom she currently resides. Her family background has shaped her into a resilient and compassionate individual who values close relationships and perseverance.

Grace completed her high school education at Saint Vincent De Paul Diocesan College, where she excelled as an honor student while actively participating in the women's basketball team. Her achievements in academics and sports showcased her discipline, teamwork, and strong work ethic.

Currently, Grace is pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology at De La Salle John Bosco College. Although IT is her second choice, she remains committed to performing well in her studies. Her true passion lies in Aircraft Maintenance Technology, a field she hopes to pursue in the future.

Beyond her career, Grace dreams of traveling the world, giving her family the comfortable life they deserve, and building her own successful café business. She believes that with determination, faith, and perseverance, these dreams can become a reality.

This is the story of who I am today—a young woman with big dreams, a passion for learning, and the determination to create a brighter future.
